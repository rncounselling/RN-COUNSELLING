Study MBBS in Kazakhstan

Discover world-class medical education opportunities in Kazakhstan with affordable fees, English medium instruction, and international recognition for Indian students.

Course Overview

Kazakhstan has emerged as a popular destination for Indian students seeking quality medical education abroad. The country offers MBBS programs that are recognized globally and provide excellent clinical training opportunities.

Duration

The MBBS program in Kazakhstan typically spans 5 to 6 years, including one year of internship. The curriculum is designed to meet international medical education standards.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) in 10+2 for General category
  • Minimum 40% marks in PCB for SC/ST/OBC categories
  • NEET qualification is mandatory for Indian students
  • Minimum age of 17 years as on 31st December of the admission year
  • Maximum age limit of 25 years for General category (30 years for SC/ST/OBC)

Medium of Instruction

Most universities in Kazakhstan offer MBBS programs in English medium, making it convenient for Indian students. Some universities also provide Russian medium courses with English language support in initial years.

Recognition and Accreditation

  • Recognized by the Medical Council of India (NMC - National Medical Commission)
  • Listed in the World Directory of Medical Schools (WDOMS)
  • Approved by WHO (World Health Organization)
  • Graduates can practice in India after clearing FMGE/NExT examination

Top Medical Universities in Kazakhstan

Kazakhstan is home to several prestigious medical universities that offer world-class MBBS education. Here are some of the top institutions:

Al-Farabi Kazakh National University

One of the oldest and most prestigious universities in Kazakhstan, offering comprehensive medical programs with modern facilities and experienced faculty.

Kazakh National Medical University

A leading medical institution known for its research excellence, advanced clinical training, and high-quality medical education programs.

Astana Medical University

Located in the capital city, this university offers state-of-the-art infrastructure, modern teaching methodologies, and excellent clinical exposure.

South Kazakhstan Medical Academy

Renowned for its practical approach to medical education, offering extensive hands-on training and clinical experience to students.

Karaganda State Medical University

A well-established institution with a strong focus on research and innovation, providing quality medical education with international standards.

West Kazakhstan Marat Ospanov State Medical University

Known for its affordable fee structure and quality education, offering comprehensive medical programs with modern teaching facilities.
