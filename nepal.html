Why Study MBBS in Nepal?

Similar Curriculum to India

Nepal follows a medical curriculum similar to India with familiar teaching methods, making it easier for Indian students to adapt and excel.

NMC Recognition

Medical degrees from recognized Nepalese universities are approved by NMC (National Medical Commission), allowing you to practice in India after clearing NEXT/FMGE.

English Medium

All medical programs are taught in English, eliminating language barriers and ensuring comprehensive understanding of medical concepts.

Proximity to India

Nepal's geographical proximity and cultural similarities make it a comfortable choice for Indian students, with easy travel access and familiar environment.

Affordable Fees

Compared to private medical colleges in India, Nepal offers quality medical education at significantly lower costs, making it accessible to more students.

Modern Facilities

Nepalese medical universities are equipped with modern infrastructure, advanced laboratories, and well-equipped hospitals for comprehensive clinical training.

Course Duration & Eligibility

Course Duration

  • Total Duration: 5 years (including internship)
  • Academic Years: 4.5 years of classroom and clinical training
  • Internship: 1 year of mandatory rotatory internship
  • Degree Awarded: MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery)

Eligibility Criteria

  • NEET qualification is mandatory for Indian students
  • Completed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology
  • Minimum 50% marks in PCB (45% for reserved categories)
  • Age criteria as per NMC guidelines (17 years minimum)
  • Valid passport for international travel

Career Opportunities After MBBS in Nepal

Global Recognition

MBBS degrees from recognized Nepalese universities are globally recognized, opening doors to international medical careers.

NEXT/FMGE Eligibility

Graduates are eligible to appear for NEXT (National Exit Test) or FMGE to practice medicine in India.

Practice in India

After clearing the licensing examination, you can practice medicine anywhere in India with full recognition.

International Opportunities

Pursue medical careers in various countries after fulfilling their respective licensing requirements.

Postgraduate Studies

Eligible for postgraduate medical programs (MD/MS) in India and abroad after clearing entrance examinations.

Research & Academics

Opportunities in medical research, teaching, and academic positions in universities and research institutions.
