Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about MBBS counselling services

When should I start MBBS counselling? ‚ñº
You should start MBBS counselling preparation immediately after NEET UG results are declared. Early preparation helps you understand the counselling process, analyze previous year cutoffs, shortlist colleges, and prepare all required documents. Our counsellors recommend starting at least 2-3 weeks before the counselling registration begins to ensure you're fully prepared.
Is counselling required for private colleges? ‚ñº
Yes, most private medical colleges participate in centralized counselling (AIQ or State quota). However, some private colleges also conduct their own management quota admissions. Professional counselling helps you understand which colleges participate in centralized counselling and guides you through both processes to maximize your admission chances.
Can I upgrade my seat later? ‚ñº
Yes, seat upgradation is possible in subsequent counselling rounds if you participate in the upgradation process. You can retain your current seat while being considered for better options in your choice list. Our Premium Package includes complete upgradation support to help you secure the best possible seat across all counselling rounds.
Do you support both AIQ and State counselling? ‚ñº
Yes, our Premium Package provides complete support for both All India Quota (AIQ) and State quota counselling. We help you understand eligibility criteria for both quotas, guide you through parallel counselling processes, and ensure you don't miss any opportunities. Our Basic Package covers AIQ counselling, while State quota support is included in the Premium Package.
