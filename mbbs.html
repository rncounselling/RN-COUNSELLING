NEET UG Counselling for Different Quotas

Quota Type Details
All India Quota (AIQ) Managed by MCC, AIQ counselling is for 15% of the total seats in government medical and dental colleges across India. Open to all NEET-qualified candidates regardless of domicile.
State Quota State authorities conduct counselling for 85% of seats in government colleges and 100% of seats in private colleges within the state. The eligibility criteria and reservation policies vary from state to state. Domicile certificate is required.
Deemed & Central Universities MCC also conducts counselling for deemed and central universities like AMU, BHU, and DU. These institutions have their own fee structures and admission criteria.
AIIMS AIIMS institutes conduct their own counselling for MBBS admissions based on NEET scores. AIIMS counselling is separate from MCC counselling.
Private Colleges Counselling for private medical colleges is conducted either by the state authorities or by the respective private college management. Fee structures are generally higher than government colleges.
