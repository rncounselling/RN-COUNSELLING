Scope of Services

Applicable to both Medical & Dental packages. End-to-end support from rank evaluation to final college reporting.

💬

24×7 Counselling Support

WhatsApp queries, timeline reminders, and quick clarifications during choice filling. Dedicated counselling dashboard for tracking timelines, document checklists, and updates.

🎯

College Selection Based on Rank & Budget

Personalized shortlisting by AIR, category rank, state domicile, and budget. Transparent analysis of realistic options, borderline possibilities, and comparative fee structures.

📁

Complete Document Guidance

Checklist for MCC and state counselling. Category certificate guidance — validity, format, and issuing authority. Special quota support: PwD, Defence, Minority, Ex-servicemen. Error prevention in uploads.

📋

Bond & Bank Guarantee Guidance

State-wise bond explanation — service duration, penalty amount, rural service requirements. Bank guarantee details — amount, validity, and bank format. Risk assessment before choice filling.

🏫

In-Depth College Analysis

Faculty strength, student-teacher ratio, university affiliation, clinical exposure, patient inflow, and internship quality. Honest discussion of strengths, limitations, and PG ecosystem.

📝

Registration Support (MCC & State)

Step-by-step guidance for MCC registration, choice locking, and state portal registrations. Timeline tracking to prevent missed deadlines and common registration mistakes.

📊

Merit List Analysis

Understanding state merit list structure, category-wise ranking, and seat allocation logic. Realistic seat probability with past trend comparison for opening and closing ranks.

🏛️

State-wise Top Government Colleges

Curated list of top government colleges per state — including underrated but strong colleges. Ranked by clinical exposure, academic stability, internship quality, and rank-range mapping.

🔄

Round-wise Choice Filling Strategy

Separate strategy for Round 1, Round 2, Mop-Up, and Stray Vacancy. Tailored to rank, category advantage, and state-specific trends. Avoids overloading unsafe choices.

✅

Complete Choice Filling for All Rounds

Customized choice lists prepared by counsellors with practical ordering to maximize upgrade chances. Dynamic updates based on seat allotment results and vacancy matrix.

⚖️

Round-to-Round Decision Support

Post-allotment counselling after each round — when to upgrade, when to freeze, risk analysis before surrendering a seat. Emotional support for students and parents under pressure.

💰

Complete Fee Structure Disclosure

Transparent breakup of tuition fees, hostel fees, caution deposits, and university charges. State-wise comparison and financial planning to avoid last-minute surprises.

🗂️

Reporting Document Guidance

State and college-specific reporting checklist — originals vs photocopies and number of sets required. Guidance on admission letters, fee receipts, and bond documents to prevent rejection.

🏠

Hostel & PG Accommodation Guidance

Government hostel availability overview, allocation rules, and cost-benefit analysis of hostel vs PG accommodation. City-specific practical insights for first-year students.

💵

Internship Stipend Information

State-wise and college-wise stipend policies — amount, mode, and regularity of payment. Comparison across government colleges and impact on living expenses and PG preparation planning.
